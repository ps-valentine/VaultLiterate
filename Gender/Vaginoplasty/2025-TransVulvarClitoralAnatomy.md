- **Figure 1.** Video QR code: cisgender and transgender clitoral anatomy. The anatomy of the clitoris and vulva created during vaginoplasty for transgender and gender diverse patients differs from that of cisgender individuals. The [linked video](https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=_VST1QZ9zAg) reviews the steps of penile inversion vaginoplasty, with a particular emphasis on the creation of the neoclitoris and neovulva. It highlights the similarities and differences in cisgender and transgender clitoral anatomy, with a detailed examination of the neurovascular components that contribute to sensation and function.
