## Summary
- "Scientific literature rarely describes persons childhood role-playing performed in order to achieve sexual pleasure (see AB/DL, ageplay). While analyzing this kind of behavior, other than sexual motivations should be taken into account."
- "The aim of the study was to explore an Internet forum for persons who feel the need to engage in childhood role-playing in order to describe behaviors related to paraphilic infantilism, motivated both sexually and non-sexually."
