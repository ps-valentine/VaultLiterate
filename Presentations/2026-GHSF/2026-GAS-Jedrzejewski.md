### Medical considerations

### Challenges for Surgery
### Post-operative Care
- Use a sports bra (no underwire) for 1 month.
- Limit strenuous physical activity for 1 month.
- Monthly self breast exams.
- Yearly exams with PCP and scheduled mammograms.
- Regular imaging to detect ruptures; usually beginning 5 years after surgery.
- "25% of BA patients will need replacement surgery within 10 years."
### Complications
- Changes in nipple sensation
- Dissatisfaction with size
