## Self-Regulation & Deescalation
- Deescalation techniques
	- Shake it off: clench and unclench your muscles. Go out and walk fast, or slow.
	- Drink something hot or cold; splash cold water on your face.
	- Take deep breaths, and exhale forcefully. It helps to say something like "WOW" or "FUCK."
	- Debrief: vent, tell your story, get support, and plan for next time.
- Self-care
	- Discussion & sharing of different people's self-care techniques.
