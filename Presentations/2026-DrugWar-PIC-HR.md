# The War on Drugs, the Prison Industrial Complex & Harm Reduction
## The War on Drugs
- Drugs aren't new!
	- Alcohol, 7,000 to 6,600 B.C.; residue found in an ancient Chinese village.
	- Hallucinogens, 8,600 to 5,600 B.C.; San Pedro Cactus fossils found in Peruvian caves.
	- Opium, 4,000 B.C.; remains of poppy seeds and opiate traces found in plaque and bones of human remains in Italy.
	- Coca, 6,000 B.C.; remains of coca leaves found in house floors and in human dental and hair remains in Nanchoc Valley, Peru.
- Drugs such as heroine and cocaine were available in pharmacies into the late 1800s.
