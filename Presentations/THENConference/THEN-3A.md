## Objectives
## What is Hermeneutic Photography?
"**Hermeneutic photography** is a qualitative research methodology to explore meaning-making in the world using interviews and photos from participants (Hagedorn, 1994)."
## What is queer joy?
"**Queer joy** is a part of an epistemological framework which argues what when research only asks particular kinds of questions then they receive the same kinds of answers, limiting insight and re-perpetuating narrow views of marginalized communities (Henricks, 2016; Shuster & Westbrook, 2022).

Queer joy is about the joyful experiences that queer people live into which sustains them in the face of oppression (Shuster & Westbrook, 2022)."
